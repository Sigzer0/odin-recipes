Pico De gallo

Description

This is a simple authentic Pico de gallo recipe you can enjoy with chips or on your plate.Takes no more than 10 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • 10 Tomatoes
  • 2 jalapeno chilis
  • 2 tbsp Salt
  • 2 limes
  • 1/2 an onion
  • 1 bunch cilantro

Steps

  1. Chop tomatoes, onion, cilantro and jalapeno. Place in a bowl
  2. Get the juice of 2 limes and pour it into the bowl.
  3. Mix it well.
  4. Add salt to taste.
