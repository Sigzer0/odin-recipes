Carne Asada

Description

Simple authentic Carne asada recipe recipe you can enjoy with a cold beer.

Ingredients

  • Package of carne asada meat. Preferrably precut.
  • Bbq grill
  • 1 tbsp Salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tbsp Seasoning salt

Steps

  1. Take the meat out of the package
  2. Turn on the gas grill to medium high heat.
  3. Mix seasoning salt and oil together and rub on the meat. Let sit for about 20 minutes
  4. Throw the meat on the grill and cook each side for roughly 5 minutes.
  5. Enjoy with a cold beer.
